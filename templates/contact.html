Online Shopping Safety Recommendations

To help you make better purchase decisions and avoid scams, we suggest following these guidelines:

Spotting Fake Reviews:

  • Look for repetitive phrases: Fake reviews often use similar wording across different products.
  • Check reviewer profiles: Be cautious of reviews from new accounts with few other reviews.
  • Analyze review timing: A surge of reviews in a short period may indicate fakes.
  • Use review analysis tools: Our system helps detect unnatural language or suspicious patterns in reviews.

Smart Shopping Practices:

  • Compare multiple sources: Cross-check reviews from various websites.
  • Prefer verified purchases: These are more likely to be authentic reviews.
  • Beware of overly positive or generic reviews: Real users typically share both pros and cons.
  • Report suspicious reviews: Help improve platforms by flagging dubious content.
